Certificates

Gift certificates

Give emotions to the dearest ones!

We offer gift certificates for all master classes, courses and co-working in our studio. Certificates are available in physical and electronic form.

When purchasing a certificate, we send you an electronic certificate to the specified mail within an hour from the moment of ordering during our working hours (Wed-Sun 12:00 - 20:00).

The physical certificate can be picked up at the studio or received by new mail.

When filling out the form, enter the buyer's data.

Certificates are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase.

Gift certificates cannot be exchanged for cash.
