Certificates and abonements

  1. The gift certificate is valid for 12 months from the date of purchase.
  2. The gift certificate cannot be exchanged for cash.
  3. Gift certificates are subject to return within the period specified by the current legislation of Ukraine, namely 14 calendar days.
  4. The gift certificate can be transferred (gifted) to third parties, or exchanged for other services of Kyiv Clay Space ceramics studio.
  5. The fact of activation of the certificate is the attendance of the first class under the certificate.
  6. If the class is canceled less than 24 hours in advance, the class will be burned on the certificate.
  7. Gift certificates are valid for the period of validity of the gift certificates specified in the certificate package. The last day of validity of the corresponding Gift Certificate is the date indicated in the package of such certificate. After the expiration of the term of validity of the gift certificate, the gift certificate loses its validity, and its holder loses the right to activate the certificate and acquire the right of claim. The face value of the gift certificate is not reimbursed (is not returned) to its holder and/or buyer, except for the cases stipulated by the current legislation of Ukraine and the conditions specified on the website.
